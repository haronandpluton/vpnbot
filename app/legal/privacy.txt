Privacy Policy

PRESENT VPN 🎁

Effective date: July 7, 2026

1. Data We Process

To operate the Telegram bot and provide VPN access, the Service may process:

• Telegram ID, username, name, and user language;
• information about orders, payments, and payment statuses;
• payment amount, currency, network, address, and transaction identifier;
• subscription data: UUID, server, status, and expiration date;
• technical logs, error information, and administrative actions;
• IP address, request time, and User-Agent when the connection page is used.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used only to:

• create and fulfill orders;
• verify payments;
• issue and renew VPN access;
• prevent abuse;
• provide technical support;
• diagnose errors and maintain Service security.

3. Traffic Data We Do Not Collect

The Service does not collect or store:

• lists of websites visited;
• internet traffic content;
• DNS requests;
• the user's internet activity history.

4. Sharing Data with Third Parties

To operate the Service, certain technical data may be processed by Telegram, hosting providers, payment services, blockchain networks, and VPN infrastructure providers.

Only the amount of data required to perform the relevant operation is shared.

5. Payment Processing by Third Parties

The Service Operator may use third-party payment providers to process cryptocurrency payments.

Information required to complete a payment is processed by those providers under their own terms of service and privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary to operate the subscription, process payments, resolve disputes, maintain security, and comply with applicable requirements.

7. User Requests

For questions about data processing, users may contact support through the /help command.

By using PRESENT VPN 🎁, the user confirms that they have read this Privacy Policy.
