Refund and Payment Cancellation Policy

PRESENT VPN 🎁

Effective date: July 7, 2026

1. General Terms

VPN access is a digital service and is provided after payment confirmation.

Once the subscription has been activated and access has been issued, payments are generally non-refundable.

2. When a Request May Be Considered

The Service Operator may consider a full or partial refund if:

• the same order was paid more than once;
• the payment was confirmed, but access was not provided due to a fault of the Service;
• the Service cannot provide the paid service for technical reasons.

A decision is made after the order and transaction have been reviewed.

3. Cryptocurrency Payment Errors

The user is responsible for verifying:

• the recipient address;
• the selected blockchain network;
• the currency and token;
• the payment amount;
• the memo or destination tag, when required.

A payment sent to the wrong address, through the wrong network, or with incorrect details may be lost and cannot be automatically recovered by the Service.

Blockchain, exchange, and wallet fees are non-refundable.

4. Underpayment, Overpayment, and Expired Orders

Underpayment does not activate an order automatically.

Overpayment does not automatically increase the subscription period.

A payment made for an expired order may require manual review.

5. How to Submit a Request

To request a review, contact support through /help and provide:

• Order ID;
• transaction txid;
• amount, currency, and network;
• payment time;
• a screenshot from the wallet or exchange.

Each request is reviewed individually.
